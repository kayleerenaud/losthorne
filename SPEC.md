# Losthorne: Last Light — Design Spec

*Distilled from the design session of Kaylee, Josiah & Malachi (2026-07-02).*

## Goal
A two-player co-op adventure game for iPhone (web app). You and a friend are warriors defending the last free village in Losthorne — a Middle Ages / mythology world, inspired by Asterix & Obelix.

## Platform
Web app, phone-first (eventually iPad/desktop). Landscape orientation always. Multiplayer via room codes (each player on their own phone — no central screen). Solo play possible.

## Camera & Controls
- Top-down, Zelda-style
- Two-finger touch: left thumb joystick, right thumb combat
- Slice (swipe) / Smash (hold) / Archery (pull back for power)
- Feel target: smooth like Genshin mobile, intuitive like Zelda
- Satchel inventory, 2-weapon quick switch, tap-through dialogue, breakaway cutscenes
- Map: outlined territories fill in as explored; buy bigger maps to reveal unknown islands

## Playable Avatars (each with a unique personal side quest)
1. Curly dirty-blonde boy, brown vest — wooden flute
2. Black ruffled-hair boy, leather jacket, silver-buckle belt
3. Girl, darker skin, big bun with bone pin, dress + fur shawl
4. Blonde girl, two Dutch braids — carries a lute
Instruments = idle/rejuvenating activities; musical avatars tame animals by playing.

## NPCs
- **Chief Bonbottom** — quest-giver
- **Dorgan** — potions grant a RANDOM temporary power; better supplies = longer effect; underwater-breathing potion
- **Erik (with a K)** — trader, never free
- **Moto Moto** — blacksmith (~100 hammer, ~300 best sword)
- **Jesse** — traveling storyteller/merchant; sings news of new islands; colorful beaded dress
- Villagers, little Tommy (kidnap quest hook)

## Economy
Start 500 coins. Bread ≈8, horse ≈500, best sword ≈300. Bridge tolls, potion supplies, animal feed, maps. Each village sells specialty armor (lake village → underwater armor).

## Health & Food
5 hearts; hunger drains half-hearts. Blueberries/turkey/fish heal; RED berries = instant death → respawn at territory edge. Multi-ingredient meal restores up to 4 hearts. +5 heart cap after Evilcorn (10 before final boss).

## Quest Structure
Chief gives tasks between bosses; each teaches the skill the next boss needs:
- Tutorial: rival village's champion, entertainment fight — chief volunteers YOU
- Fire-making village → Troll (light)
- Rescue shipwrecked traders → swimming → Kraken
- Farmer's runaway animal → taming → horse
- Negotiation quests with other villages
- Each avatar has one unique personal side quest

## Enemies
Goblins, wisps, bears, piranhas. Giant wolf packs — kill all within a time window or survivors summon MORE. Health bars appear on first hit. Death → respawn at territory edge.

## Bosses
1. **Witch** — mini-boss
2. **Mountain Troll** — fought in dark; defeat by light / survive to daytime. Reward: treasure
3. **Barbarian knight** — kidnaps Tommy; rides two-headed horse; win WITHOUT killing horse → keep it
4. **Lake Kraken** — cut off each arm; needs Trident (3 trials: archery, swimming, kindness; MUST return it or war). Beak → special shield
5. **Evilcorn** — cursed unicorn; heal it → +5 hearts
6. **FINAL: Shapeshifting Wizard** — summons creatures, shifts through harder forms, ends as dragon; then one final blow

## Art
Puss in Boots: The Last Wish style — painterly, NOT pixelated, medieval, darker mythological palette.
